RUNNERS — GOBLINS & GOOD GUYS
Lore: The Neon Sump & Goblin Origins
The goblins are descendants of an illegal late-20th-century corporate bio-engineering project that went rogue around the same time the ELIZA mainframe first glitched out. Originally engineered as hyper-agile low-maintenance industrial workers designed to clean toxic waste lines, they were completely abandoned when the corporate grid collapsed into the grimy un-optimized neon sprawl the city is today.
They live in The Neon Sump — a subterranean network of forgotten subway tunnels, abandoned sewer mains, and illegal basement labs directly beneath the potholes and city bus routes. They have built a vibrant chaotic punk culture down there, scavenging scrap metal and bootleg tech from the surface. They strip copper wire from anything after ingesting their special blue gemstones, which give them super energy.
Zee (starting goblin girl, purple pigtails) is a native of the Sump. Her natural agility and small stature make her the ideal test subject for sprinting through the hazardous broken streets above to scavenge gems and tech parts.
